01 — The problem

One click is all it takes

Phishing pages, fake logins, and malicious QR codes look identical to the real thing. By the time a link finishes loading, your passwords or money can already be gone.

02 — How it works

Paste a link or scan a QR code. Sandboxed opens it in a disposable browser far from your device, follows every redirect, screenshots the real page, and scores the risk — then tells you in plain language exactly why.
